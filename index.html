Minesweeper

Mines: 10
Time: 000

Click a cell to start the game!

How to Play:

  • Left-click a cell to reveal it
  • Right-click a cell to flag/unflag it as a suspected mine
  • Numbers show how many mines are adjacent to that cell
  • Empty cells automatically reveal surrounding safe areas
  • Win: Flag all mines and reveal all safe cells
  • Lose: Click on a mine
